Belgian Entrepreneurs

Odoo was founded by Belgian entrepreneurs Fabien Pinckaers and Xavier Blanc in 2005. In 2002, Pinckaers started working on an open-source ERP system called TinyERP. He continued to develop the software and eventually renamed it Odoo.

From OpenERP to Odoo

Odoo started as OpenERP, a software company that provided open-source enterprise resource planning (ERP) solutions. The company then rebranded to Odoo in 2014 to reflect its expanded product offerings. Today, Odoo is a comprehensive suite of business applications that includes ERP, CRM, project management, and more. The company has grown rapidly and now has a presence in over 100 countries, serving millions of users worldwide.

Open-source and SaaS

Odoo offers its software as open-source, with a commercial license for enterprise customers. The company also provides a SaaS (Software as a Service) version of its software, where customers can subscribe to use the software hosted by Odoo.

Emphasis on Simplicity and Customizability

Odoo differentiates itself from other business software solutions by offering a user-friendly interface and emphasizing simplicity and customizability. The company provides a modular approach, allowing customers to choose and implement only the features they need, and to add new features as their business grows.
