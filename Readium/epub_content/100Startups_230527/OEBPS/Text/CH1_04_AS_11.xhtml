Founding Team

Traveloka was founded by Derianto Kusuma, Ferry Unardi and Albert Zhang in 2012. Kusuma and Unardi were former employees of McKinsey & Company, while Zhang was a former software engineer.

Journey to Success

Traveloka began as a travel search engine and gradually expanded into a one-stop online travel booking platform. It has since become one of the leading online travel companies in Southeast Asia, offering flights, hotels, and other travel-related services. Today, Traveloka operates in multiple countries in the region and has been recognized for its innovation and growth.

Revenue Generation

Traveloka operates on a commission-based model, earning a percentage of the total booking value for every transaction made through its platform. In addition, it also generates revenue through the sale of in-platform advertising and the provision of value-added services such as travel insurance.

Understanding the Market

Traveloka recognized the opportunity to digitize the travel industry in Southeast Asia, where many traditional travel agencies were still operating in a traditional, offline manner. By offering a convenient, online platform for booking travel, it has been able to capture a significant share of the market and grow rapidly.

Major Funding History of Traveloka

Traveloka has received funding from a number of well-known investors, including East Ventures, Expedia, and Sequoia Capital. To date, the company has raised over $700 million in investment and is valued at over $1 billion.
