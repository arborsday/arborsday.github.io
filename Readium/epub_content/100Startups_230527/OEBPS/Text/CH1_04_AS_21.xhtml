Co-Founders with Experience in Cybersecurity

Transmit Security was founded in 2014 by veterans in the cybersecurity industry, Mickey Boodaei and Rakesh K. Loonkar. Mickey Boodaei previously co-founded and served as CTO at Portal Solutions, a cybersecurity company that was later acquired by RSA Security. Rakesh K. Loonkar served as CTO of RSA Security for the EMEA region after the acquisition of Portal Solutions.

Providing Identity Management Solutions for the Financial Services Industry

Transmit Security has established itself as a leading provider of identity management solutions for the financial services industry. The company's solutions are designed to simplify and streamline the process of identity verification and authentication, while ensuring the highest levels of security.

B2B SaaS Model Focused on Financial Services

Transmit Security operates on a B2B SaaS model, providing its identity management solutions to financial services organizations such as banks, payment providers, and insurance companies. The company generates revenue by selling its solutions to these organizations and offering ongoing support and maintenance services.

Bridging the Gap between Convenience and Security

Transmit Security's identity management solutions aim to bridge the gap between convenience and security in the financial services industry. With the increasing need for seamless and secure online transactions, the company's solutions provide financial organizations with the tools to meet these demands while maintaining the highest standards of security.
