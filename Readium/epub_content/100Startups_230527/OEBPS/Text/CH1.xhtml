Chapter 1.
Unicorn startups around the world

A unicorn startup is a company with a valuation over $1 billion. The term appeared in 2013 by venture capitalist Aileen Lee, choosing the mythical animal to represent the statistical rarity of such successful ventures.

In this chapter, we will take a look at 100 companies that have risen to become unicorn startups around the world. Corporate valuations will fluctuate greatly from 2023, but please understand that this information was selected based on performance and data from 2020 onwards.
