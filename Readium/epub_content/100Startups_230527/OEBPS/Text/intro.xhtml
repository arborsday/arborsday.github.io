Intro

The world is wide, and as wide as the world is, innovation is happening all over the world. Do you know how much innovation is happening around the world? I was curious about that while working on a startup business. This is because when we think of startups, we often only think of Silicon Valley, or we often only know about Israel and India.

In this book, you can see at a glance the stories of great startups who have achieved unicorns around the world. Startups in each country have developed in a way that suits the country's environment to solve the problems needed in that country. It will be interesting to see why startups from all over the world were born, grew, and even became unicorns.
