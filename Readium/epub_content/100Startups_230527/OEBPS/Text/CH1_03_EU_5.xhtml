Experienced Entrepreneurs

Cabify was founded by three experienced entrepreneurs: Adrian Merino, Francisco Montero and Juan De Antonio. They saw the need for a more efficient and reliable transportation option in Spanish cities and decided to start Cabify in 2011.

Ride-Hailing and Delivery Services

Cabify operates as a ride-hailing service, connecting passengers with drivers through its mobile app. It also offers delivery services for businesses and individuals, allowing customers to send packages and items quickly and efficiently. Cabify makes money by taking a commission on each ride or delivery made through its platform.

Responding to Market Needs

Cabify has been successful in the Latin American market by responding to the needs and demands of the market. The company provides a high-quality and reliable transportation option in cities where traditional taxis are often unreliable. It also offers premium services that cater to a more discerning customer base. By understanding the market and responding to its needs, Cabify has been able to establish itself as a leader in the Latin American ride-hailing industry.
