The Pioneers Behind Checkout.com

Checkout.com was founded in 2012 by Guillaume Pousaz. The founder had a vision to create a payment platform that would simplify and streamline the checkout process for merchants and consumers.

Transforming the Payment Industry

Checkout.com started as a payment solution for online merchants, offering a range of payment options and a user-friendly interface. The company quickly gained popularity and has since grown into a leading payment platform, serving thousands of merchants worldwide. Checkout.com has been recognized for its advanced technology, security, and customer support.

Monetizing through Merchant Processing Fees

Checkout.com makes money by charging merchants a fee for processing their payments. The company offers a range of payment options and provides a secure, reliable, and efficient payment platform for its merchants.

Innovating in the Payment Space

Checkout.com has transformed the payment industry by simplifying and streamlining the checkout process for merchants and consumers. The company has attracted investment from top venture capital firms and has a growing merchant base, serving thousands of businesses worldwide. Checkout.com is also a leader in the payment space, constantly innovating and expanding its offerings to provide the best possible experience for its customers.

Major Funding History of Checkout.com

Checkout.com has received substantial investment from top venture capital firms, including Insight Partners and Coatue Management. The company has raised over $830 million in funding, with its latest valuation estimated to be around $15 billion.
